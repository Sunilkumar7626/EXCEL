# 🚲 Bike Sales Dashboard (Excel Project)

## Overview
This project analyzes customer purchasing behavior for bike sales using Microsoft Excel.  
The goal is to identify key factors influencing bike purchases and build an interactive dashboard for business decision-making.

The analysis includes data cleaning, KPI calculation, pivot table analysis, and dashboard creation.

---

## Objectives
- Analyze bike purchase behavior
- Identify customer segments with high purchase probability
- Discover trends based on income, age, and commute distance
- Build an interactive dashboard for business insights

---

The dataset contains customer demographic and behavioral data with 1000 records.

---

## Dashboard KPIs
- **Total Customers:** 1000
- **Bikes Purchased:** 481
- **Purchase Rate:** 48%
- **Average Income:** $56,360

---

## Key Insights

### 1. Strong Market Demand
48% of customers purchased a bike, showing significant market potential.

### 2. Income Impacts Purchase Decisions
Customers with higher income are more likely to purchase bikes.

### 3. Middle-Aged Customers Are Core Buyers
Customers aged 31–54 represent the highest bike purchase segment.

### 4. Commute Distance Matters
Customers with short-to-medium commute distances show higher purchase behavior.
